你自己的書庫Your own library

你已經擁有的內容，原地就能讀。 Read what you already own, right where it lives.

GoManga 主打兩種最單純的閱讀來源——你自架的 Komga 伺服器，與你裝置上的檔案。其餘來源由你自行設定。 GoManga leads with the two simplest sources — the Komga server you host and the files on your device. Everything else is yours to configure.

定位很清楚Where we stand

GoManga 是什麼——以及不是什麼。 What GoManga is — and what it is not.

GoManga 是閱讀器與來源設定框架，不代管漫畫內容。來源、檔案與存取權限都由你自行設定與負責。 GoManga is a reader and a source-configuration framework. It hosts no manga content — the sources, files, and access rights are yours to configure and own.

  • 讀你擁有的書庫：你自架的 Komga、本機檔案，以及你自行設定的來源——全部由你掌握。Reads the library you own: your self-hosted Komga, local files, and the sources you configure — all in your hands.
  • 不代管內容：不上傳、不販售、不散佈漫畫，也沒有官方內容目錄。Hosts no content: no uploading, selling, or distributing manga, and no official catalog.
  • 本機優先：書庫、進度、清單與偏好主要保存在你的裝置上。Local-first: your library, progress, lists, and preferences live mainly on your device.
  • 不推薦來源：不提供來源清單或內容索引，也不協助繞過登入或付費。Recommends no sources: no source lists or content indexes, and no help bypassing logins or paywalls.
  • 不追蹤你：本網站不加入第三方分析、廣告 SDK 或行為追蹤。Doesn't track you: no third-party analytics, ad SDKs, or behavioral tracking on this site.
  • 不取代你的判斷：你需確認自己有權存取與使用相關內容。Doesn't replace your judgment: you confirm you have the right to access and use the content.
